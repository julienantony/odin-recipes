PAROTTA

Bun Parota Recipe is a popular dish in madurai. It is puffy parota which are cooked till crispy and it is fluffy inside.

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make batter with the ingredients
  2. Roll the batter in round
  3. Put it in Tawa
  4. Serve and Eat
